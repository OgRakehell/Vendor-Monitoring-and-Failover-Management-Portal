##  System Architecture (High-Level)

From a product standpoint, the architecture separates decision-making, execution, and visibility.

### Transaction Processing Layer
- Handles customer requests and emits transaction outcomes.

- Vendor Integration Layer
Abstracts vendor-specific APIs behind a common interface.

- Monitoring & Analytics Engine
Aggregates logs, computes metrics, and tracks trends.

- Failover & Decision Engine
Applies health rules and controls routing automatically.

- Vendor Management Portal
Provides visibility, control, and auditability.

- SLA & Reporting Module
Translates operational data into business insights.
